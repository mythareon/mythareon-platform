# Business Strategy

## Vision
Mythareon is the foundational control plane for AI quality, reliability, cost, and governance. Starting with evaluation and regression testing for LLM teams, expanding into observability, cost optimization, governance, and verticalized AI solutions.

## Market Opportunity
- **TAM**: $50B+ AI operations and governance market
- **SAM**: $5-10B LLM evaluation + observability + cost management
- **Target**: Engineering teams, AI product managers, ML ops teams at 100-20K person companies

## Competitive Positioning
**Axes of Differentiation:**
1. **Multi-layer control** — Not just evals, but observability + cost + governance in one platform
2. **Developer-first** — Built for engineers, not just stakeholders
3. **Full stack** — From model selection through production monitoring through cost optimization
4. **Platform, not point solution** — Scales from eval to full AI operations

## Go-to-Market
**Phase 1 (Months 1-3):** Bottom-up through eval product, target LLM engineering teams, viral loop through model comparison results

**Phase 2 (Months 4-6):** Observability module, add production monitoring, expand to AI ops teams

**Phase 3+ (Months 7+):** Cost control, governance, vertical solutions (finance, healthcare, customer service AI)

## Business Model
- **Freemium SaaS** — Free tier for individuals/hobby projects
- **Usage-based pricing** — Pay per eval run, per model comparison, per token monitored
- **Enterprise plans** — Custom pricing for governance + compliance + support

## Key Metrics (North Star)
- Monthly active evals
- Production AI systems under monitoring
- Cost savings generated
- Customer expansion into secondary products
