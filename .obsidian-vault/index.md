## Core Platform

**Mythareon** helps teams evaluate, compare, and improve AI systems in production. The platform starts with structured evaluation workflows and expands into observability, cost optimization, governance, and verticalized solutions.

### Key Capabilities
1. **Eval & Regression Testing** — Compare prompt/model changes before release
2. **Observability** — Track AI behavior and quality in production
3. **Cost Control** — Monitor and optimize token usage and model selection
4. **Governance** — Manage access, policies, and compliance
5. **Vertical Solutions** — Industry-specific AI workflows
